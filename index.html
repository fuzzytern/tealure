A unique taste from Ilam District, Nepal.

It is in the pristine highlands of the eastern region of Nepal that small scale farmers cultivate and make one of the purest tea on Earth. By working directly with some of these family-owned plantations, Tealure delivers the fruits of their work straight to your home, ensuring utmost quality.

» Get introduced to the farmers.

Their tea, your impact.

Everytime you purchase tea via Tealure, 20% of that cost goes directly to the farm who made it.
In addition, as a tribute for the remarkable work conducted by the farmers, Tealure uses 10% of its profit to support the rural communities in the Ilam district with access to education and main amenities.
